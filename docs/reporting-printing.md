# Reporting & Printing

### Overview

The AKMA platform includes robust reporting and printing capabilities to ensure that stakeholders have accurate, up‑to‑date information. Users can produce internal operational reports and external documents (purchase orders, BoQs, invoices, warehouse receipts) in both Arabic and English. Reports can be filtered by project, date range and category.

### Standard reports

* **Project dashboards** — High‑level dashboards summarising project progress, budget versus actual spending, outstanding purchase orders, stock levels and key financial metrics. Project managers can filter by project, cost category and date period.
* **Procurement reports** — Listings of purchase requests awaiting approval, purchase orders by supplier, delivery statuses, outstanding goods receipts and supplier invoices.
* **Warehouse reports** — Current stock balances, stock movements, receipts and issues per warehouse or item, transfer history and suggested re‑order quantities based on minimum stock thresholds.
* **Financial reports** — Statements of income and expenses, accounts payable and receivable, cash flow reports and lists of advances and loans. These can be filtered by project or cost centre.

### Printouts and document templates

The system provides built‑in print templates for key business documents:

- **Purchase orders** — Includes supplier details, order items (description, quantity, unit price), delivery instructions and signature blocks. A QR code or reference number can be included for tracking.
- **BoQ reports** — Summarises BoQ items, units, quantities, unit prices and total amounts, with revision history and progress to date.
- **Goods receipt vouchers** — Records the quantities received against a purchase order, with space for notes and acknowledgement signatures.
- **Inventory issue vouchers** — Documents items issued from a warehouse to a site or project, including item codes, quantities and purpose.
- **Invoices** — Generates supplier or client invoices with line items, tax calculations, payment terms and notes.

All templates support bilingual labels and automatically populate the company logo, project name, document date and reference numbers. Administrators can customise logos, footers, fonts and whether amounts display VAT or other taxes.

### Export & sharing

Reports and printouts can be exported to PDF or Excel for archiving and sharing outside the system. Users can download documents, print directly from the browser or email them to stakeholders. Access rights control who can view, generate or export each report or document.

### Custom reports

Advanced users or administrators can create custom reports by combining fields across modules. Filters and grouping options allow building pivot‑style summaries (e.g., expenses by project and month). Custom reports can be saved and reused.

These reporting and printing features ensure transparency and provide a reliable audit trail across all modules of the AKMA platform.
