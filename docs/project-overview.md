# Project overview

## Background

In the construction and finishing industry, contractors juggle many simultaneous jobs, suppliers and financial obligations.  
Spreadsheets, handwritten notes and disparate tools make it hard to track costs, materials and progress. Procurement processes are manual and inconsistent.  
Management lacks a single source of truth for projects, bills of quantities (BoQ), warehousing and finance, leading to budget overruns and disputes.

## Purpose of the system

The AKMA Platform aims to centralise operational workflows into a unified, bilingual web application.  
It streamlines project setup, BoQ creation, procurement, warehousing, finance and reporting.  
By capturing every transaction, the platform provides real‑time visibility on costs, outstanding payments and materials on site.  
It enforces approval workflows, ensures data consistency and generates professional Arabic/English reports that can be shared with stakeholders.

## Target users

- **Project managers** – oversee jobs, define BoQ items, approve requests and monitor progress.  
- **Procurement officers** – raise purchase requests, compare supplier offers, issue purchase orders and track delivery.  
- **Warehouse staff** – manage stock in/out, record material usage and report remaining quantities.  
- **Accountants** – record income, expenses and payments; reconcile invoices; produce financial statements.  
- **Management & consultants** – analyse dashboards, extract reports, enforce permissions and audit workflows.

## Benefits

- **Increased efficiency** – eliminates duplication of data entry and reduces manual paperwork.  
- **Visibility & control** – real‑time dashboards for material consumption, expenditures and outstanding invoices.  
- **Reduced errors** – standardised processes and validations prevent common mistakes (e.g. duplicate orders).  
- **Faster decision making** – comprehensive reports enable managers to spot trends and make informed decisions.  
- **Scalability** – the modular design allows the system to grow with the business, adding new modules when needed.
